Welding Equiptment

Your welding equipment is one of the most important things as you start your journey, it does not have to cost you a small fortune but good quality equipment can make a world of difference. This is why we have provided some suggestions below to get you started, as a beginner with all the options out there it can be hard to know what to get. Health and safety in this industry is key as it is a hazardous hobbie to have if the right precautions are not taken.
